Roohan school is a rich history and commitment to education.
Founded 2003 we've been a cornerstone of our community for 5 years. Our mission is to nurture holistic development, foster academic excellence, and create a safe, supportive environment. Meet our exceptional team of educators, explore our diverse curriculum, and join our Roohan school community as we inspire and empower the leaders of tomorrow.Thank you for taking the time to learn more about Roohan school. If you have any further questions or would like to connect with us, please don't hesitate to reach out.

At Roohan Public School our mission is clear: to provide a holistic and enriching educational experience that empowers students to achieve academic success while nurturing their character, creativity, and curiosity. Our values of mention values guide everything we do, from our innovative teaching methods to our commitment to inclusivity and diversity.

We take immense pride in our exceptional team of educators, administrators, and support staff. Our dedicated professionals bring a wealth of experience, enthusiasm, and dedication to creating a positive and nurturing learning environment for every student.

Academic excellence is at the core of what we do. Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to challenge and inspire students, preparing them for a successful future. We offer a wide range of subjects, extracurricular activities, and support services to cater to each student's unique needs and talents.

We understand the importance of providing a safe and supportive environment for our students. Roohan public school is committed to creating a space where students feel valued, respected, and encouraged to thrive academically and personally.

We invite you to become a part of the Roohan public school community. Whether you're a prospective student, a parent, or simply interested in learning more about us, we welcome your interest and involvement. Together, we can continue to shape the future of education and inspire the leaders of tomorrow.
